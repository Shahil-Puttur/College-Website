Our Management & Vision

Vidyarashmi group of institutions is spread across 25 acres on the peak of Vidyagangothri. You can spot the well set campus on 16th K.M. in the adjacent of Puttur-Kukke Subrahmanya state high way. The great visionary Mr. K. Seetharama Rai Savanoor initiated this venture in the year 2001 in commemoration of his father late Mr. Sheentoor Narayana Rai, who was a soldier, farmer and a teacher.

Experienced teachers coach kindergarten students. A dedicated scientifically raised building houses the kindergarten students. The kids love to come and dwell here. The education is backed by advanced technologies and each child is monitored with individual attention. The Karnataka State syllabus in English Medium in the content of education for primary and high schools. Coming to plus two level of education we have Vidyarashmi First Grade College B.Com. and B.A. degrees. Vidyarashmi First Grade College is the result core of the initiative taken by SNR Rural Education trust. It’s based on the E. Malloy’s word “The college degree is not a sign that one is finished product. But an indication that a person is prepared for life. In the year 2001 SNR Rural Education Trust started it’s yeomen service in rural Savanoor to peruse local youth to get basic and degree education.
