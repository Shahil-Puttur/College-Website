The Bachelor of Arts Program

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) program at Vidyarashmi First Grade College, Savanoor, offers a strong foundation in humanities and social sciences. The course helps students develop critical thinking, communication skills, and a broad understanding of literature, history, sociology, and other disciplines. It prepares graduates for diverse career opportunities in education, public service, media, research, and higher studies.

Critical Thinking

Analyze complex social and historical contexts with a sharp mind.

Communication Skills

Master the art of effective communication for media and public service.

Broad Understanding

Explore diverse disciplines from literature to sociology for a holistic view.
