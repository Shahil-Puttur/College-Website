Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Explore the diverse world of Humanities and Social Sciences

The Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) program at Vidyarashmi First Grade College offers a rich and diverse curriculum that explores history, economics, political science, sociology, and literature. This course is designed to develop critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and communication skills. Our students are prepared for a wide range of careers in civil services, journalism, teaching, social work, and further academic research, empowering them to become informed and engaged citizens.

Diverse Subjects

Study a broad range of topics from history to economics and literature.

Critical Thinking

Develop strong analytical and intellectual reasoning skills.

Versatile Careers

Opens doors to careers in civil services, media, education, and more.
