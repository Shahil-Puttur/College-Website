Red Cross Unit

Promoting Health, Humanity, and Hope

❤️ Red Cross Unit – Vidyarashmi First Grade College, Savanoor

The Red Cross Unit of our college promotes humanitarian values and social responsibility among students. It organizes activities such as health awareness programs, blood donation camps, first-aid training, and disaster relief support. The unit encourages students to serve society with compassion, care, and a sense of duty toward humanity.

Our Mission in Action

Our goal is to create a generation of volunteers who are prepared to help those in need, promoting health and well-being within the campus and beyond.
